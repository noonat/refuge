Keys: arrow keys to aim, x to shoot
Only one shot at a time!
Keep the invaders away from your city.
When the city is destroyed, all is lost.
Shoot invaders for points.
Earn bonuses with tricky shots.

Refuge is a game developed for Ludum Dare 15. The theme for the compo was "caverns".

The game was developed in 48-hours using flixel and sfxr.
